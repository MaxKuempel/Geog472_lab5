Mineral Industries of Select Countries in the Indo-Pacific

Data counts number of mineral industries within territorial land borders. Offshore oil was excluded. Mineral industries include mining and processing facilities. Countries selected are: Bangladesh, Bhutan, Brunei, Burma, Fiji, Malaysia, Mongolia, Nauru, New Caledonia, New Zealand, Papua New Guinea, Philippines, Singapore, Solomon Islands, South Korea (Republic of Korea), Sri Lanka, Taiwan, Timor-Leste, and Vietnam.

Data from: https://www.sciencebase.gov/catalog/item/65caa1aed34ef4b119cb3427 Country geojson from: https://geojson-maps.kyd.au/
